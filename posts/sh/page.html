Overview

During my time at Shopify, I got the opportunity to work with the home team on the admin dashboard. I was in charge of several aspects of the Home page from redesigning key UI elements to implementing new ones. I also led stakeholder meetings to clearly communicate the design goals and specifications to PMs, developers, content strategists and designers, making sure the projects were aligned with overall strategy and product direction.

Understanding merchant goals

Every day half a million merchants go to their home page to plan their day. Some of those merchants have full-time jobs and have very little to spare on their store while others consider their shop as the one and only source of revenue. Those merchants have very specific needs. They are definitely not your common social media users! They look for tangible content that will impact their store.
Home delivers this content through actionable cards that that are rank by a recommender system.Furthermore sticking to the following design principles help Home to always benefit the merchants.
