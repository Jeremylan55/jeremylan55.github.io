The EngPlay Experiece

Overview

EngPlay is a termly theatre production put on by the University of Waterloo Engineering Society. With auditions open to anyone and everyone, the play changes frequently in both scope and topic. Some shows have been written by the directors for the term, while other shows have been purchased scripts from professional playwrights. There has been the occasional musical, but all EngPlay's share a common theme: laughter. We love goofing around and having a good time and getting people to join in on the fun with us. The production can change drastically from night to night based on improv, audience interaction, and last-minute plot twists. EngPlay is a tradition shared amongst many schools, and one that sits close to Waterloo’s heart.

My Role

I was contacted by Engplay's directors to design a poster for their upcoming play satirically titled "The Planet of the Arts". What was initially supposed to be a Saturday evening job quickly turned out to be the complete marketing of the play, design of the logistic, art direction and branding.

Poster Design

The university of waterloo is flooded with posters, how do we make our stand out? I decided to follow the following criterion to achieve

  • Clever/memorable idea skinned with nice aesthetics.
  • Intriguing 15 metres away as it is 5 centimetres away.
  • Builds curiosity and enables the viewer to follow up.

Web Design

I also helped to design and code their website from scratch. We knew that most people coming to the site would be from seeing the poster. The posters are a key part of the site, they communicate the identity of the event. I made sure that the UI was such that it would match any future posters.
